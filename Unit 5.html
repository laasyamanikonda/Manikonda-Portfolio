Unit 5

What Is This?

This project, similar to Unit 4, is an informational and interactive slideshow about reading. It contains information and guidance about why people should read, how to pick a good book, where/where not to look for recommendations, etc.

What Did I Learn? What Does It Show?

For this project, we were given the option to choose what kind of project we wanted to do, which was a great opportunity for me to explore my creativity, as well as figure out how to incorporate CS concepts. I love both CS & reading, so I was able to bring together 2 of my passions in this project. This motivated me to formulate a great project, and was probably my best unit project.

How was it created

This project was created through several conditionals, onKeyPress, onKeyHold, etc. onMousePress was also essential to switch through pages, as well as groups, which made switching through pages incredibly efficient. Specifically, I used onKeyPress to create a label everytime a key is pressed.
